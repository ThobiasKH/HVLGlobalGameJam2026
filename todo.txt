Mask puzzle platformer where masks change gameplay, e.g.: 

Mask of stone 
- Makes you heavy 
- Pressure plates will activate when stood on
- Ice no longer slides 

Mask of wind
- Slide until hitting walls 
- Can cross gaps 
- Cannot activate pressure plates 

Mask of fire
- Can light stuff 
- Can endure heat
- Water kills you 

and so on..
